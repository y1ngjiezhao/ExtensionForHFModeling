
Invtsim.py is designed for order-update (decision) simulation. The Simulation file is for original Inventory performance, namely, BASE.
And BU, MinT, TDFP are for decision reconciliation.
Other files are for obtaining the average performance across levels, i.e. holding costs, backlogging cost, achieved service level, total cost, Forecasting Method and Decision Reconciliation Scenarios.
